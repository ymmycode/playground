About

Hi, I'm Achmat Fauzi, the developer behind this web application welcoming you. Why did I make this website? Well, back then I come to the idea of nostalgia when I look at the playground near my home. Then I thought, "what if I create digital recreation about playing or chilling at a playground that makes people feel nostalgic and peaceful?", so I make this simple web application.
